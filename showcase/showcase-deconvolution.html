deconvolution

Heavy hitters from Switzerland! With two releases under their belt they are one of the most promising European bands in the next wave of Heavy Hardcore. Keep an eye on these boys for sure. Deconvolution first released their trash influenced hardcore to to the world in 2017 with ‘Fool’s Path’. A great string of shows follow with bands like Expire, Terror and Broken teeth. 2019 marked their second a release and they’re ready to take it to the next level with two brand new tracks.
