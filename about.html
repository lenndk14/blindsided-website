who are we?

Blindsided Records is a record label based in Delft, the Netherlands. Founded in 2018 by Peter Schoor together with life-long friend Connor Boshuijer, they released the first EP on the label in the same year; “Gravestepper” by Deathtrap. While looking for other bands to recruit, they hosted their very first event for the release of “Gravestepper” together with bands such as Hawser (NL), Lowest Creature (SWE), Dagger Threat (GER) and Voidcrawler (NL). It was here when the public first got a taste of what Blindsided Records is; a unstoppable force of heavy music.

discover

Our primary goal is to introduce some of Europe's hardest hitting bands, whether it's punk, metal, hardcore or polka. We know our way around the music and know what we want to hear and see in a band, and try to convey just that to our listener. We sincerely hope that through our label people will find a new artist on the heavy music spectrum that they can call their favourite.

releases

  • “Maddening Descent” by Voidcrawler
  • “Split” by Voidcrawler and Deconvolution
