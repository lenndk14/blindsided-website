we are blindsided

We are a record label based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. Since 2018 we have been fixated on releasing the freshest, most hard-hitting riffs from the European mainland. We are mainly focused on the Dutch hardcore/metal scene but are always keeping our eyes open for gems from over the Dutch border.

Vinyl, Tape and CD

We believe a high quality physical product still is a very important thing. Listening to a record on your turntable is the most personal and romantic way to experience music, so we do our best to create a product we, the artist, and you will be happy with.
